"12 Months of Flowers"

For this project, the objective was to show 12 months of a topic. My topic was 12 months of flowers that are in season. For each clip, it shows what type of flower it is and the month it is likely to bloom in. I gathered clips from Youtube and used Adobe Premier Pro to create the timeline video.
